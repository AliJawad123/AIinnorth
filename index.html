Welcome AI

Gilgit-Baltistan, a mountainous region in northern Pakistan, is gradually embracing artificial intelligence (AI) technology. The local government and private businesses are investing in AI to improve various sectors, such as healthcare, education, and tourism. For instance, AI-powered diagnostic tools are being used in hospitals to assist doctors in diagnosing illnesses, and AI-based educational software is being deployed in schools to improve learning outcomes. In addition, AI-powered chatbots are being developed to facilitate communication with tourists, and drone technology is being utilized to monitor wildlife and natural resources.
